Details

Friday, July 10

There will be a casual get-together & rehearsal dinner at the venue. All are welcome to attend.

The ceremony will begin at 6:30 PM.

Attire

We kindly request that our guests wear cocktail attire. Keep in mind that the venue is outdoors and the event is in the middle of July. We suggest comfortable shoes and lighter fabrics. There will be cold water and chilled wine in equal measures to keep you comfortable.

Lodging & Transportation

We have secured room blocks at two local hotels (information below). Transportation will be provided from the hotel to the venue and back on July 11. Uber & Lyft are also options if you prefer. If you plan to drink, we kindly request that you do not drive.
